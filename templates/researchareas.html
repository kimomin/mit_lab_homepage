Rresearch Areas

Biomedical Signals

We focus on advanced signal processing and machine learning techniques for ECG, EEG, and PPG to extract meaningful physiological insights and support clinical decision-making.

Medical Imaging

We develop innovative computer vision and deep learning algorithms for medical imaging, including X-ray, CT, and MRI, to improve diagnostic accuracy and workflow efficiency.

Electronic Health Records

We leverage large-scale electronic health records to develop predictive models and data-driven solutions that enhance patient care, risk assessment, and personalized medicine.
